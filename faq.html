Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting properties

How do I apply for a rental property?

To apply for a rental property, you'll need to fill out our online application form, provide proof of income, references, and a security deposit. Our team will review your application within 24-48 hours and contact you with the results.

What documents do I need to rent a property?

You'll typically need: government-issued ID, proof of income (pay stubs or bank statements), rental history, references, and a credit report. Some properties may require additional documentation.

How much is the security deposit?

Security deposits typically range from one to two months' rent, depending on the property and your rental history. This deposit is refundable at the end of your lease, minus any damages or outstanding charges.

Can I have pets in the rental property?

Pet policies vary by property. Some properties are pet-friendly with additional pet deposits, while others may not allow pets. Please check the specific property listing for pet policies or contact us for details.

What utilities are included in the rent?

Utility inclusion varies by property. Some properties include water and trash, while others may include all utilities. Check the property listing for specific details about what's included in your rent.

How do I report maintenance issues?

You can report maintenance issues through our online portal, by calling our maintenance hotline, or by contacting your property manager directly. Emergency issues are handled 24/7.

What is the lease term?

Most of our properties offer 12-month leases, but we also have options for 6-month and month-to-month leases. Some properties may offer flexible lease terms - contact us for specific details.

Can I sublet my rental property?

Subletting policies vary by property and lease agreement. In most cases, you'll need written permission from the landlord. Please review your lease agreement or contact us for specific policies.

How do I renew my lease?

We'll contact you 60 days before your lease expires to discuss renewal options. You can also contact us directly to initiate the renewal process. Renewal terms and rent adjustments will be clearly communicated.

What happens if I need to break my lease early?

Early lease termination policies vary by property. You may be responsible for rent until a new tenant is found, plus any associated fees. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation and options.
